# Accessibility Features for Students with Reading Impairments

## Text-to-Speech (TTS) Support

StudyStuart includes comprehensive audio support to help students with reading impairments:

### Features:

1. **Audio Control Button**
   - Located in the top-right corner of every screen
   - Blue circle icon indicates audio is enabled
   - Gray circle icon indicates audio is disabled
   - Animated pulse effect when speaking
   - Tap to access audio settings

2. **Automatic Text Reading**
   - Welcome messages are read automatically
   - Screen transitions announce the new context
   - Questions and answers can be read aloud
   - Feedback messages are spoken

3. **Manual Reading Controls**
   - Speaker icons next to important text
   - Tap any speaker icon to hear that specific text
   - Questions have dedicated speaker buttons
   - Answer options have individual speaker buttons

4. **Audio Settings**
   - Enable/disable audio globally
   - Settings persist across app sessions
   - Easy toggle from any screen

### How to Use:

1. **Enable/Disable Audio:**
   - Tap the audio button in the top-right corner
   - Toggle the "Enable Audio" switch
   - Audio preference is saved automatically

2. **Listen to Specific Text:**

   - Tap speaker icons on answer options to hear them
   - Feedback is spoken after each answer
   - Final scores are announced

### Technical Details:

- Uses Flutter TTS (Text-to-Speech) plugin
- Speech rate: 0.5 (slower for better comprehension)
- Language: English (US)
- Volume: 100%
- Pitch: Normal

### Benefits for Students:

- **Independent Learning:** Students can navigate without assistance
- **Reduced Reading Stress:** Audio support reduces cognitive load
- **Multi-sensory Learning:** Combines visual and auditory learning
- **Inclusive Design:** Works for all students, not just those with impairments
- **Confidence Building:** Students can verify their understanding by listening
